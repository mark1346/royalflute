Lesson Philosophy & Pricing

A studio shaped around the student.

— Five Tenets —

What guides the studio

  1. I

    Customized one-on-one lessons recognizing each student’s unique strengths, weaknesses, and learning styles.

  2. II

    Personalized guidance and adaptive teaching strategy to maximize the student’s progress.

  3. III

    Emphasis on technical proficiency, musical expression, and artistry — the three pillars that develop a well-rounded flutist.

  4. IV

    A collaborative relationship between teacher and student where both parties contribute to the learning process.

  5. V

    Encouraging self-directed learning habits — instilling self-reflection skills and practice techniques for independent work outside of lessons.
