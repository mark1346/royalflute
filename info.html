Lesson Goals

A studio built around the student

  • Customized one-on-one lessons recognizing each student’s unique strengths, weaknesses, and learning styles.
  • Personalized guidance and adaptive teaching strategy to maximize the student’s progress.
  • Emphasis on technical proficiency, musical expression, and artistry to develop a well-rounded flutist.
  • Collaborative relationship between teacher and student where both parties contribute to the learning process.
  • Encouraging self-directed learning habits by instilling self-reflection skills and practice techniques for individual practice outside of lessons.
