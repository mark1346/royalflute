About the studio

A studio shaped around the student.

The Studio

One-to-one, shaped to the student.

Royal Flute Studio is a 1:1 private flute studio located two blocks from Santana Row, Santa Clara, CA. Open to teaching students of all levels and ages — our youngest student is seven.

The studio holds two recitals each year, and every student is given a performance opportunity. Lesson frequency is customisable to each student’s pace, and a strong personalised music profile is built for each one.

Students travel from across Silicon Valley and the wider Bay Area — including Cupertino, San Jose, Sunnyvale, Mountain View, Los Altos, Saratoga, Los Gatos, and Campbell.

“Every student possesses distinct qualities and talents, and I am dedicated to recognising and nurturing these traits throughout your music journey.”

— Five Tenets —

What guides the studio

  1. I

    Customised one-on-one lessons recognising each student’s unique strengths, weaknesses, and learning styles.

  2. II

    Personalised guidance and adaptive teaching strategy to maximise the student’s progress.

  3. III

    Emphasis on technical proficiency, musical expression, and artistry — the three pillars that develop a well-rounded flutist.

  4. IV

    A collaborative relationship between teacher and student where both parties contribute to the learning process.

  5. V

    Encouraging self-directed learning habits — instilling self-reflection skills and practice techniques for independent work outside of lessons.
